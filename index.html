Assignment 1: Literature Review

For the first assignment of this module, I had to choose from a list of topics to produce a literature review on the chosen topic. I chose to focus on the use of Machine Learning (ML) in retail shop floor stock management. I believed this would suit what I’m currently studying and would be something that’s familiar for me. The aim of the literature review was to evaluate the current state of ML technologies being implemented into shop floor stock management and compare them to traditional methods. It was to also highlight opportunities and challenges that come with implementing such technologies.
